# TC-052 – Verify City field behavior with special characters

## 🧪 Test Steps

1. Open Add New Address form  
2. Fill all required fields with valid data except City  
3. Locate the "City" field  
4. Enter special characters only (e.g., @#$%&)  
5. Click on "Submit"  

---

## ✅ Expected Result

- Invalid special characters are rejected, or
- Validation error message is displayed
