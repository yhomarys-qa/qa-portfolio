# TC-008 – Verify decreasing product quantity in cart

## 🧪 Test Steps
1. Open the application  
2. Log in with valid credentials  
3. Add a product to the cart  
4. Navigate to the cart page  
5. Increase quantity (optional, e.g., to 2)  
6. Decrease the product quantity (e.g., from 2 to 1)  

---

## ✅ Expected Result
- Product quantity decreases correctly  
- Total price is updated accordingly  
- Quantity does not go below the minimum allowed (e.g., 1)  
- Changes are reflected immediately  
- No errors occur during the update
