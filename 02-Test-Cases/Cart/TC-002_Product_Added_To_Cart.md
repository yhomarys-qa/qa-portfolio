# TC-002 – Verify product is added to cart

## 🧪 Test Steps
1. Open the application  
2. Log in with valid credentials  
3. Navigate to the Product Catalog page  
4. Select any product  
5. Click on "Add to Basket" button  
6. Click on the cart icon to open the cart page  

---

## ✅ Expected Result

The selected product is displayed in the cart
