# TC-013 - Validate Total Price Update

## 🧪 Test Steps
1. Open the application
2. Log in with valid user credentials
3. Add a product to the shopping cart
4. Navigate to the shopping cart page
5. Change the quantity of the added product
6. Verify the total price displayed in the cart

---

## ✅ Expected Result

The system should automatically update the total price according to the updated product quantity
