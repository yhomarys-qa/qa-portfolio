# TC-010 – Verify total price calculation based on product quantity

## 🧪 Test Steps
1. Open the application  
2. Log in with valid credentials  
3. Navigate to the Product Catalog  
4. Select a product and note its unit price  
5. Add the product to the cart  
6. Go to the cart page  
7. Increase the product quantity (e.g., from 1 to 3)  

---

## ✅ Expected Result
- Total price is correctly calculated as (unit price × quantity)  
- Updated total is displayed immediately after quantity change
