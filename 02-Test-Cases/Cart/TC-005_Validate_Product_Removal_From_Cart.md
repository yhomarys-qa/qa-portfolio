# TC-005 – Validate Product Removal From Cart

## 🧪 Test Steps
1. Open the application
2. Log in with valid user credentials
3. Add a product to the shopping cart
4. Navigate to the shopping cart page
5. Click on the remove/delete button for the added product

---

## ✅ Expected Result

The selected product should be removed from the shopping cart and should no longer appear in the cart contents
