# TC-007 - Validate Search Result Product Price to Unauthenticated Users

## 🧪 Test Steps
1. Open the search field
2. Enter a valid product name (example: "apple")
3. Press the Enter key
4. Observe the product price displayed in the search results

---

## ✅ Expected Result

The product price must be displayed clearly with currency information and associated with the correct product (example: 10.99$)
